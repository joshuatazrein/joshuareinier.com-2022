fleeing the dead we have done this deed
and forever after we dream
infirm of addled fads ended
of all these dreary days found and ended before a sadness descends
forgotten about or justly denied
full in falling demented fanged energy
fended off after lead threatens its fanged defense 
felt after dark's threaded finger alters the drunkenness to forgery
inciphered fonts thrashing heavied essence
the flutter of leaves in wind's tendrils
flushed from form and altered in sense
and falling down, the flattenedness
flees its colossal defense, its orphaned ephraim sledding to embers
forwards singed for art's leadership
the falcons descend; the flesh aroused by lessening flees its drop
infirm addition to the farthest indirection
indefensible, unadulated, unfeared, incensed
is found altered forever
is in flight; is after; is dead
